Interesting problem.

You can

1. Render the entire scene to a texture (TexA)
2. Render TexA to the window
3. Render only the section of TexA that surrounds the bat's face to the bottom left of the window.

https://wiki.libsdl.org/SDL_CreateTexture

You would create TexA with the SDL_TEXTUREACCESS_TARGET flag, then render to it with

SDL_SetRenderTarget: https://wiki.libsdl.org/SDL_SetRenderTarget

To go back to the window as the render target, you would just call SDL_SetRenderTarget(renderer, NULL)
